# Partner Account Manager (PAM) User Guide

## Overview

As a Partner Account Manager (PAM), you are the primary owner of partner relationships throughout the entire onboarding journey. You have full visibility into all gates and are responsible for coordinating with other team members (PDM, TPM, PSM, TAM) to ensure successful partner launches.

## Your Responsibilities

- Own the partner relationship from pre-contract through post-launch
- Complete Pre-Contract PDM Engagement questionnaire
- Coordinate Gate 0: Onboarding Kickoff
- Oversee all gate progressions
- Ensure timely completion of questionnaires at each gate
- Monitor partner progress and identify blockers
- Maintain partner records and documentation

## Getting Started

### Logging In

1. Navigate to the Partner Onboarding Hub URL
2. Click "Log In" in the top right corner
3. Use your Netlify Identity credentials
4. You'll be redirected to the dashboard

### Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard displays:

- **All Partners**: You see all partners you own across all gates
- **Gate Distribution**: Visual breakdown of partners by current gate
- **Recent Activity**: Latest questionnaire submissions and gate completions
- **Quick Actions**: Links to create new partners or complete questionnaires

## Working with Partners

### Creating a New Partner

1. Click "Add New Partner" on the dashboard
2. Fill in partner details:
   - Partner name
   - Your name (PAM owner)
   - Contract type (PPA, Distribution, Sales-Agent, Other)
   - Tier classification (Tier 0, 1, or 2)
   - CCV (Contractually Committed Value)
   - Target launch date
3. Click "Create Partner"

### Viewing Partner Details

1. Click on a partner card from the dashboard
2. View comprehensive partner information:
   - Team assignments (PAM, PDM, TPM, PSM, TAM)
   - Contract details and timeline
   - Gate progression status
   - Completed and pending questionnaires
   - Gate approval signatures

## Pre-Contract Phase

### Pre-Contract PDM Engagement Questionnaire

**Purpose**: Validate whether a partner qualifies for PDM support before contract signature.

**When to Complete**: Before contract signature, when considering PDM engagement

**Sections**:

1. **Executive Sponsorship Confirmed**
   - Verify executive-level commitment from partner
   - Document sponsor name and title

2. **Commercial Framework Alignment**
   - Confirm partnership structure is defined
   - Validate revenue model alignment

3. **Technical Feasibility / Partner Operational Questions**
   - Identify key technical questions
   - Document operational concerns

4. **Near-Term Closure Timeline**
   - Confirm 60-day closure timeline
   - Document target contract date

5. **Strategic Partner Classification**
   - Verify Tier 0/1 classification
   - Confirm CCV meets threshold (≥10% of Country LRP)

**How to Complete**:

1. Navigate to Questionnaires → Pre-Contract PDM
2. Fill out all five sections
3. Review pass/fail status for each section
4. Provide digital signature
5. Submit questionnaire

**Pass Criteria**: All five sections must pass for PDM engagement approval

## Gate 0: Onboarding Kickoff

**Purpose**: Validate readiness for white-glove onboarding support

**When to Complete**: After contract signature, before beginning onboarding

**Key Criteria**:
- Contract execution complete
- Partner team identified and committed
- Launch timing within 12 months
- Financial bar met (CCV ≥$50M for Tier 0 auto-qualification)
- Strategic value validated
- Operational readiness confirmed

**Your Role**:
- Coordinate with partner to complete questionnaire
- Ensure all criteria are met
- Obtain necessary approvals
- Sign off on gate completion

## Gate 1: Ready to Sell (Weeks 1-12)

**Purpose**: Partner team trained and ready to sell Kuiper services

**Phases**:
- **Phase 1A**: Onboarding Kickoff & Planning (Weeks 1-3)
- **Phase 1B**: GTM Strategy & Technical Discovery (Weeks 3-6)
- **Phase 1C**: Training & Enablement (Weeks 7-12)

**Your Role**:
- Work closely with PDM during this phase
- Ensure GTM strategy is approved
- Verify sales team certification
- Monitor training completion

**Key Deliverables**:
- Project plan approved
- GTM strategy approved
- Technical architecture defined
- Sales team certified

## Gate 2: Ready to Order (Weeks 13-17)

**Purpose**: Systems integration and operational processes complete

**Phases**:
- **Phase 2A**: Systems Integration & API Implementation
- **Phase 2B**: Operational Process Setup

**Your Role**:
- Coordinate with TPM for technical integration
- Monitor API integration progress
- Ensure operational processes are established

**Key Deliverables**:
- API integration complete
- Monitoring active
- Test transactions successful

## Gate 3: Ready to Deliver (Weeks 18-20)

**Purpose**: Operational readiness and launch validation

**Phases**:
- **Phase 3A**: Operational Readiness (Weeks 18-19)
- **Phase 3B**: Launch Validation (Week 20)

**Your Role**:
- Coordinate with PSM and TAM
- Verify beta testing completion
- Ensure support transition is complete
- Sign off on launch readiness

**Key Deliverables**:
- Beta testing successful
- Support transition complete
- Operational metrics validated

## Post-Launch

**Your Role**:
- Monitor partner performance
- Coordinate ongoing support with TAM
- Track success metrics
- Identify expansion opportunities

## Best Practices

### Communication

- Keep all team members informed of partner status
- Document important decisions in partner notes
- Escalate blockers promptly
- Schedule regular check-ins with partners

### Timeline Management

- Review partner timelines weekly
- Identify at-risk partners early
- Adjust target dates as needed
- Communicate delays to stakeholders

### Documentation

- Complete questionnaires thoroughly
- Provide detailed notes for failures
- Keep partner records up to date
- Document all gate approvals

### Collaboration

- Engage PDM early in pre-contract phase
- Coordinate with TPM for technical gates
- Work with PSM/TAM for launch and post-launch
- Leverage documentation hub for resources

## Common Scenarios

### Scenario: Partner Fails Gate Criteria

1. Review failure reasons in section status
2. Work with partner to address gaps
3. Consult with relevant team member (PDM, TPM, PSM, TAM)
4. Re-submit questionnaire when ready
5. Document lessons learned

### Scenario: Partner Timeline Slipping

1. Review current gate status
2. Identify specific blockers
3. Adjust target launch date if needed
4. Communicate with stakeholders
5. Create action plan to get back on track

### Scenario: Need to Reassign Team Members

1. Navigate to partner detail page
2. Click "Edit Partner"
3. Update team assignments
4. Save changes
5. Notify affected team members

## Reports and Analytics

1. Navigate to Reports page
2. Apply desired filters
3. Click "Export to CSV"
4. Open in Excel or Google Sheets

## Troubleshooting

### Cannot Submit Questionnaire

- Ensure all required fields are completed
- Check that signature is provided
- Verify network connection
- Try refreshing the page

### Partner Not Appearing in Dashboard

- Verify you are listed as PAM owner
- Check that partner was created successfully
- Try logging out and back in
- Contact support if issue persists

### Gate Progression Blocked

- Review previous gate status
- Ensure all criteria are met
- Check for failure reasons
- Complete any pending questionnaires
